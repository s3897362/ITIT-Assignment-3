Individual Reflection

Aryan Verma

The communication of the group went swiftly, through using teams and discord we were able to communicate with each other efficiently and clearly, while also getting closer with each other and understanding each other as friends. This helped the group environment since we were not strangers with each other and were more like friends, all contributing to a group assignment. Further the content to the group work was proof-read by group members to ensure that the information was relevant to the rubric. Improvement could be pre-planning meetings to ensure that all members are able to come to meetings, pre planning the work during the first week of the assignment would also be efficient, so group members know which parts of the assignment they are doing. This will allow for more time to do the assignment especially when there is such a time specific assignment and will ensure for more time to edit the information when everyone is done with their content. The extension was surprising however, very helpful. Without the extension the work which we produced would not have as much depth and content as what we do now. Overall, the extension provided more time to complete the assignment to a higher standard.

Group Reflection

Throughout the assignment process it was agreed that the communication was key for the group. Throughout the extensive use of discord and teams group members were able to understand each other as friends and group members, we understood that work needed to be complete by certain dates and everyone attempted their hardest to complete their assigned parts by the given due date. The peer feedback was also a highlight throughout the project, on google docs we created a system in which peers could review work and verify it when proof-read, a highlight system was used to ensure that information was precise and accurate and was completed to the highest standard possible, and covered all points on the rubric. All in all everyone contributed equally and completed their given parts at the highest possible standard and informed group members on teams and discord their progress and how long it would take for them to complete. However there were also downsides to this group project, one key area was the time, since we started late there was a lot of stress people were under due to the timeframe. It was agreed through members that meetings could be planned 24 hours in advance to ensure that everyone would be able to attend the meeting, since it was shown through the meetings that some people could not attend meetings due to work and or other commitments. Furthermore planning and assigning given parts to people could have been done earlier to ensure that people were able to complete their parts before the set due date. This would have been very efficient for the group since members would be able to understand which parts they are doing, when it is due by and allows each person to research more about their given section. Something which was unexpected was the extension given by Anthony, this was crucial for our group since it gave us a reality check on where we should be, given that there was only about a week left. The report and website would have rushed, however the extension allowed for more depth and content to go into the report, and the website is cleaner and more simplistic, portraying the report in a format which stands out to the user. To conclude it was agreed that, time played a key factor in this assignment and to improve on this, time management should be planned out along with given parts of the assignment to ensure that work is complete.
