    Group Processes

    For assignment 2 the group has worked efficiently in producing a completed report of our project idea and personal information. This report was completed within the allocated time and was submitted without any issues. Meetings have occurred weekly throughout the time span of the assignment. The team has cooperated tremendously and there have been no issues between the members. All members helped each other out and have provided full support towards the team's goals.
    Although for assignment 3 there will be some significant changes to be implemented. Such as organising tasks between members as soon as possible and using the experience we have gained from report writing in assignment 2, we will be attempting to complete assignment 3 well before the submission deadline. Most of the group skills would be implemented in assignment 3 as well such as cooperation and communication. The tasks would be delegated according to each member's abilities and areas of expertise, which should in turn make the writing of the report much more efficient and effective. The team would also try to get every single member present for all meetings as this would allow everyone to clarify their questions and can allow the team to progress together without any members left behind on information.
    These improvements to our work flow during assignment 3 would hopefully allow us to finish earlier than the submission date and allow us to progress much faster while retaining maximum quality over the report and prototype.
