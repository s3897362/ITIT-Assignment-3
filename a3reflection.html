Group Reflection

Throughout the assignment period the group has cooperated well and has completed all required sections in the time that was assigned. All group members have contributed equally and have attended most of the meetings. The meetings were organised to provide every member the chance to join and contribute or clarify any details. Another thing that went well was that the group had finished all components required for the final report which gave us time to work on the prototype and work on the issues that were faced during the planning and testing processes. With some quick thinking we quickly pivoted to alternative solutions to the problems we encountered and restructured some areas of our project but overall gave our project greater feasibility.

What could have been improved would be to have more resources readily available and organised code which had been implemented to work with our project idea to its exact form so that we could complete the prototype which would have allowed us to complete the prototype without any issues smoothly.
