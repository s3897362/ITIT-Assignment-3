A Day in the Life of an IT Pro: IT Manager

Jonathan Dalia works as an IT manager for Langer Transport Corporation. Dalia is responsible for configuring, setting up and maintaining voice, video and data connections at the company's several locations. Dalia is also responsible for troubleshooting any issues that may present themselves. Additionally, Dalia oversees company report writing and interacts with the SQL database. As an IT manager, he often interacts with the other company employees who may be having certain issues with the company systems. Dalia primarily works from the company's head office in New Jersey and can interact with systems in other locations via "redundant internet connections". Fixing issues remotely at other company locations pose a slight challenge for Dalia as he cannot physically be present to see what the issues are, which makes solving them more difficult. However, Dalia relies on employees at each of the other locations to be his "eyes and hands", making his job easier.
